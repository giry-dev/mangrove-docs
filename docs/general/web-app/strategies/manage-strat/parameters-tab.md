# Parameters tab

import useBaseUrl from '@docusaurus/useBaseUrl';

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/assets/parameters_tab.png')} width="60%"/><br /><br />

Your strategy can have one of three statuses: active, inactive, or closed. Depending on the status, alerts may be displayed.

## Information cards

You can find descriptions of these in the [Overview tab](./overview-tab.md#information-cards).

* Unrealized PnL
* Total base/quote inventory

## Editing Parameters


<img src={useBaseUrl('img/assets/parameters_edit.png')} width="60%"/><br /><br />

By clicking on the Edit Parameters button, you are able to edit your price range, inventory, settings and bounty.


<img src={useBaseUrl('img/assets/parameters_edit_2.png')} width="60%"/><br /><br />


Once you have selected the desired values press the Summary button and Proceed with the changes. Keep in mind, that any time you edit your parameters, the strategy will be reset and liquidity will be published again.
